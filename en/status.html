What I'm up to

What I'm Learning

Ever since I started making games I've used Unity 3D, even if I tried some different options like Godot and GameMaker Studio. This year I decided to expand my horizons a little bit more and learn Unreal Engine, UE5 specifically.

What I'm Playing

Some years ago I played Yakuza: 0 as my first game in the series, not much later I got hooked. Yakuza: Like a Dragon has all the landmarks that I love from the series: a melodramatic over the top main story, goofy side-quests, and addictive mini-games to get lost for hours. The game's main departure from the series is the change of genre, going from a brawler to a turn based RPG. This change didn't bother me the least, and I've been enjoying this game a bunch.

What I'm Practicing

Aside from my professional skills, there are 3 skills I'm trying to improve currently: chess, boxing and Guilty Gear: Strive. I've been improving at chess for a while and I don't plan on stoppin soon, my current goal is to reach 1500 Elo in chess.com. This year I started practicing both boxing a Guilty Gear, I liked them quite a bit so I to continue improving in them. For Guilty Gear my current goal is to reach the "celestial floor", altough it's been a little hard for me since it is my first tradicional fighting game. A for boxing goes, I have no current goal but to simply improve both my endurance and technique while having fun.
