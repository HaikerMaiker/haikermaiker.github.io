About Me

I love Videogames

Since I started playing when I was a little kid, I fell in love with videogames and the experiences they brought me. Be it playing with my friends or exploring vast mysterious worlds by myself, I still have the same passion for videogames that I had when I was little. This does not mean that I don't enjoy other stuff like going to the cinema, watching TV series or playing board games with friends and family, but my true passion will always be for videogames.

Unrelenting passion to Improve and Learn

I'm always learning and improving at something, be it at my professional skills, chess, some sport or a competitive game. I truly believe that overcoming yourself yields a satisfaction like no other, no matter in which aspect of your life. I show some of this stuff that I'm trying to learn/improve in the "What I'm up to" section of this page.

I have fun Solving problems

I have fun thinking about how to solve problems, whether they're in code, a project of mine, or a game. I believe that every problem has a solution, even if it seems impossible at first. I concentrate the most when I'm looking for these solutions, and I can do it for hours on end. This has led me to become quite capable at doing so.
